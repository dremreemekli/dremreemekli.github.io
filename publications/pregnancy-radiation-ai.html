Assessing the Accuracy, Readability and Understandability of Websites, ChatGPT, Copilot, and Bard Answers on the Radiation during Pregnancy

Authors: Burcu Mert, Emre Emekli

Why is this study important?

With the rise of large language models (LLMs) such as ChatGPT, Copilot, and Bard, many patients now turn to AI chatbots for health-related information. However, while these tools can provide immediate answers, there are growing concerns about their medical accuracy, readability, and ability to be understood by a general audience. This study investigates how reliable these tools are in addressing one of the most sensitive medical topics: radiation exposure during pregnancy. Our findings help highlight both the potential and limitations of AI in patient education.
