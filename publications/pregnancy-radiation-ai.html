Assessing the Accuracy, Readability and Understandability of AI Answers on Radiation During Pregnancy

Burcu Mert, Emre Emekli

Why This Study Matters

This research evaluates how well modern AI tools like ChatGPT, Bard, and Copilot perform in delivering medically accurate and patient-friendly information about radiation exposure during pregnancy. With more patients turning to the internet and AI for medical advice, ensuring these tools provide trustworthy and understandable information is critical. This study not only assesses accuracy but also focuses on readability and understandability, key factors in effective communication between healthcare systems and the public.
